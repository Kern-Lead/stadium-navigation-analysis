    Proposed Hybrid Navigation Architecture

    A balanced approach that restores essential B2B patterns while maintaining the simplicity benefits of V3.

    Design Philosophy

    Hybrid Approach Goal

    Restore Solutions-first navigation for enterprise buyers while maintaining V3's clean user experience through progressive disclosure and smart information architecture.

    What We Keep

    • Clean, uncluttered visual design
    • Simplified mobile experience
    • Fast decision-making paths
    • Reduced cognitive load

    What We Restore

    • Solutions dropdown (B2B standard)
    • Team-based navigation paths
    • Use case scenario discovery
    • Platform feature visibility

    Proposed Navigation Structure

    Platform | Solutions | Catalog | Resources | Pricing

    Key Change: Solutions Dropdown Restored

    Re-introduces the Solutions dropdown as the primary navigation for enterprise buyers, organized by Team, Use Case, and Industry to meet B2B software expectations.
