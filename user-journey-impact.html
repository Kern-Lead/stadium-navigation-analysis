    The Core Problem

    Enterprise buyers expect to find solutions organized by their business context (team, use case, industry). When this organization is removed or buried, they struggle to connect their needs with Stadium's offerings, potentially leading to higher bounce rates and lower conversion.

    Recovery Strategy

    To repair broken user journeys while maintaining V3's simplification benefits:

    1
    Restore Solutions Dropdown: Re-establish the primary enterprise navigation pattern with By Team, By Use Case, and By Industry organization to repair broken buyer journeys.
    2
    Reconnect Teams to Solutions: Move team-based navigation from Resources back to Solutions context where enterprise users expect to find it, restoring the connection between audience and solutions.
    3
    Add Progressive Disclosure: Use smart information architecture to surface clean, simple entry points while providing deeper navigation options for users who need them.
